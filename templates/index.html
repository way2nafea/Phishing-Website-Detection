HOW NEXUS WORKS

Every URL passes through our five-layer security pipeline in under two seconds.

01
INPUT & NORMALIZE

URL is parsed, normalized, and domain components extracted for analysis. Protocol, path, and parameter patterns are flagged immediately.

02
MULTI-ENGINE ANALYSIS

DNS resolution, SSL validation, domain age lookup, Google Safe Browsing query, and ML model inference all run in parallel against the target.

03
THREAT VERDICT

Results are fused into a risk score. SAFE, SUSPICIOUS, or PHISHING verdict is delivered with a confidence score and detailed breakdown.
